Frequently asked questions


How do I book?

Go to our website RubyDubyGlam.com download our app and register your personal information. Once completed you are ready to book an appointment.

Do I need an iPhone to book an appointment?

No. You can also book an appointment through our website.

How long is a blowout appointment?

Blowout appointments usually take about an hour.

How long is a Makeup appointment?

Makeup appointments are about an hour.

How long is an Updo appointment?

Updos can take up to an hour to an hour and a half.

How long is an eye extension appointment?

Full sets average two to two and a half hours. Fills are about an hour.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

For Blowouts please wash hair within the hour of the stylists arrival.

For Updos, there is no need to shampoo hair.

For Makeup, please have clean and moisturized face.

For Updos, there is no need to shampoo hair.

For Makeup, please have clean and moisturized face.

The assigned stylist will send you a message an hour prior to your appointment notifying you know that they are in route.

Do I need any products?

No all products are provided by the stylist.
